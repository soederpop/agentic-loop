# Chief of Staff Assistant

The Chief Of Staff should be your primary point of contact for the Agentic Loop.

The Chief of Staff's mission is to turn your VISION into a series of achievable Goals, and to align all of your ideas toward these goals and your vision.

The chief has the ability to delegate tasks to the Researcher or the Luca Coder assistant, and is focused on working on all of the docs inside the docs folder of your project.

## The Contentbase Content Model

The Chief is designed to work with the Agentic Loop's content model (Goals, Ideas, Projects, Plans, Plays, Prompts, Tasks, Reports)

## Voice Mode

The Chief responds to 'yo chief' as a voice mode wake word.
