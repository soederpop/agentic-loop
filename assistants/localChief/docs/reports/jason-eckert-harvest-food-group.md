## Synthesis

Jason Eckert appears to be the co-founder and longtime leader of Harvest Food Group, a Chicago-area frozen food company founded in 1999. The strongest public source reviewed says he co-founded the company with his father and remained CEO at the time of Harvest's acquisition by Industrial Opportunity Partners in May 2023.

Harvest Food Group appears to operate as a frozen-food manufacturer and supply partner rather than simply as a consumer-facing brand house. Its business spans co-manufacturing, private label, ingredients, IQF solutions, foodservice, packaging, R and D, and distribution. The company presents itself as having serious operational capability, including a USDA-inspected and SQF-certified facility in Chicago, with headquarters in Naperville.

The clearest consumer brand associated with Harvest Food Group is Path of Life, which markets plant-based frozen foods such as vegetable blends, grain blends, sides, and meal-oriented products. In this quick pass, Path of Life was the only clearly identified in-house retail brand, although Harvest likely supports many third-party products through private-label and co-manufacturing relationships.

The most important current development around the company is the 2023 acquisition by Industrial Opportunity Partners. There is also evidence from search results that Amit Pandhi may have become president and CEO later in 2023, suggesting that Jason Eckert's leadership role may have changed after the acquisition. That later leadership update appears credible, but would benefit from direct confirmation if needed.

Overall, the online picture is that Jason Eckert helped build Harvest Food Group into a meaningful frozen-food platform with both B2B manufacturing capabilities and at least one visible consumer brand. The company appears active, operational, and growth-oriented, with the acquisition representing a major milestone in its trajectory.
