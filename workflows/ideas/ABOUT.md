# Ideas

A dedicated browser for viewing all of your ideas. Filter by status, goal, or tags. Click into any idea to read its full content. Includes a built-in chat panel for talking to any assistant (defaults to Chief of Staff) and a status dashboard showing goals, projects, and recent activity.

## Features

- **Browse & filter** — search, status filters, goal filter, tag filter, keyboard navigation
- **Chat** — talk to any assistant directly from the ideas view (press `c` to toggle). Default: Chief of Staff
- **Status** — quick dashboard showing idea counts, goals, projects, and recent commits (press `s` to toggle)

## When to use

Use this when the user wants to see their ideas, browse through them, search or filter, read the full content of a specific idea, or chat with an assistant about their ideas and next steps.

## Trigger signals

- "Show me my ideas"
- "What ideas do I have?"
- "Let me look at my ideas"
- "Browse ideas"
- "What's in exploring status?"
- "Show me ideas tagged with..."
- "I want to chat about my ideas"

## When NOT to use

- If the user wants to capture a new idea — use **Capture** instead
- If the user wants to develop/flesh out an idea — use **Shape** instead
